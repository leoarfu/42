Frequency Bands: Ka, Ku, V & E — Tradeoffs

MODULE 4
RF & LINK BUDGETS

Frequency is the most fundamental parameter in satellite link design. It determines path loss, antenna size, available bandwidth, atmospheric susceptibility, and regulatory regime — all simultaneously. This lesson maps the progression from L-band to E-band, explains why Ka-band dominates LEO broadband, establishes the path-loss vs frequency numbers, and adds the band-comparison function to the spine project.
